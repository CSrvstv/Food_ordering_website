About Us

PIZZA PLAZA introduced fresh and a innovative approach to pizza by offering pizza lovers over 20 different delicious toppings that are richer and more sumptuous than those of our competitors, as well as a selection of great tasting cheese blends and inviting appetizers to enhance the total pizza experience. Our ingredients matter. Whether it's our signature sauce, toppings, our original fresh dough, or even the box itself, we invest in our ingredients to ensure that we always give you the finest quality pizza. The most essential ingredient for great tasting pizzas and pastas is the highest quality cheese. At Pizza Plaza, our superb blends of mozzarella and cheddar cheese are made by using 100% pure milk. At PIZZA PLAZA, we don't just make pizza. We make people happy.
